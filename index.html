The Philippine Islands is an archipelago of 7,641 islands in Southeast Asia. This tropical country is blessed with captivating landmarks, capturing its natural beauty.





Boracay, Malay, Aklan

A tropical paradise with powdery white-sand beaches, clear turquoise waters, and vibrant nightlife in the Philippines.




Big Lagoon, El Nido, Palawan

A serene paradise of emerald waters surrounded by towering limestone cliffs, offering breathtaking natural beauty and tranquility.




Fortune Island, Nasugbu, Batangas

An enchanting destination featuring ancient Greek-inspired ruins against a backdrop of azure waters.
